Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

At RapidSubmit.net™, we prioritize the privacy and security of the data you submit through our indexing terminal. This policy outlines how we handle information during the submission process.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the specific data points required to execute your submission request:

  • Submission Metadata: Website titles and URLs provided in the submission form.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses and timestamps to prevent system abuse and ensure API stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Indexing Execution: Transmitting your URL to the IndexNow API (Bing, Yandex, etc.).
  • Social Signaling: Sending your metadata to authorized webhooks for social media distribution.
  • Service Improvement: Monitoring submission success rates to maintain high delivery speeds.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

RapidSubmit.net does not sell or rent your personal data to marketers. Data is shared only with the third-party platforms you select during submission (e.g., Search Engines and Social Networks). These third parties have their own independent privacy policies governing how they handle the links once received.

4. Data Retention

Submission logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days to facilitate troubleshooting and prevent spam, after which they are automatically purged from our temporary cache.

5. Security Measures

All data transmitted through RapidSubmit is protected via Industry-Standard SSL (HTTPS) Encryption. This ensures that the connection between your browser and our submission engine remains private and secure.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to inquire about the data processed during your session. Given the transient nature of our indexing service, most data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently.
